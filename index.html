Mind Reading Trick

Follow the instructions Given Below the Numbers, click the button and see if I can predict the symbol you are thinking of. It will appear in the dark square.

Instructions

  1. Select any two digit number.
  2. Add the two digits together, and subtract this from your starting number.*
  3. Using this final answer, look up the magic symbol on the table above.
  4. Concentrate very hard on this symbol.
  5. Click the Read my mind button!

*e.g. If your number was 51, then 5 + 1 = 6. Subtract 6 from 51 to get your symbol at number 45.
